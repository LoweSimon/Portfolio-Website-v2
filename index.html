What you can expect when working with me

A website created in three simple stages

The Design Stage

Following the initial contact, we will arrange a meeting either over Zoom or an in person coffee chat.
We will discuss your business requirements. What you want out of your website. The functional requirements of your website.

After we have an agreement, I will create some initial designs. Then once you are happy, I will move on to the Build Stage, creating your website in your chosen framework.

The Build Stage

Once designs are approved, I will begin creating your website, keeping you up to date with progress. I will also keep you in the loop, keeping you involved in any testing along the way, as I feel keeping you involved is an important step in building relationships and ensuring you get exactly what you want out of this.

Once I have a fully working website, approved by you, we will move on over to the Handover Stage.

The Handover Stage

The Handover Stage is about giving you full access to your own website. There will be training involved in ensuring you understand how the website works, and how you can maintain it (WordPress sites).
Once this has been complete, you will have full ownership of your website.

However, I don’t just disappear there. I will endeavour to keep an ongoing relationship to ensure your website stays up to date in this ever changing technological world. As who is better to work on your website, than the original developer.
